Who are we ??

We are SPART one of the latest tech teams of IIT Bombay! We are a bunch of enthusiastic students striving to increase the cult of airships within insti and the world. Well, airships are not unknown to the world. They have been around since the 1850s, but a very renowned incident, the Hindenburg disaster, almost ended the journey of these striking beauties in the 20th century. Airships are massive lighter-than-air systems that look like oval-shaped balloons but have engines to give them power and control their direction of movement. We believe one day, airships will be admired and much acknowledged owing to their glorious capabilities. They are more efficient and environmentally friendly than any modern-day air vehicle, promoting sustainable air transport. Also, we seek the golden opportunity to proudly represent IIT Bombay on an international platform by competing in the WSAR (World Solar Airship Race) 2023.
